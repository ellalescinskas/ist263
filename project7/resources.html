My Personal Volunteer Story

Growing up, I have been involved with the nonprofit Best Buddies International, an organization close to my heart. I have taken part in their Friendship Programs since a young age, fostering friendships with individuals with IDD, helping break social barriers and promoting inclusion.

I also volunteer for the Martin Richard Foundation Challenger Sports Program, teaching young adults and children with disabilities to play sports. This program is very special to me and has allowed me to build lifelong friendships, discover new passions, and strengthen my leadership skills.
